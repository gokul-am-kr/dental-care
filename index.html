About Us

Hello, We Are Here
For Your Smile

Chempakasseril Dental Care is a multispecialty dental clinic located in Pallipad, Haripad. We welcome you to a whole new world of dentistry designed to make your smile better.

A modern practice, we believe your smile is our pride. Our skilled team of dental professionals is committed to providing you with an exceptional experience from start to finish, offering comprehensive services from all fields of dentistry.

  • Guaranteed treatment satisfaction
  • Best-in-class treatment technology
  • Highest quality implants and materials
  • Complimentary oral health consultation
